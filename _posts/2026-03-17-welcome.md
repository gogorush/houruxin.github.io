Hey there! If you're reading this, chances are we speak the same language. Not English, not Mandarin – I'm talking about **code**.

I'm Hou, a software engineering veteran who's spent way too many hours debugging at 3 AM, falling down rabbit holes on Hacker News, and getting genuinely excited about new programming languages (yes, I'm that person).

## Why This Blog Exists

Look, the tech world moves fast. *Really* fast. Some days it feels like drinking from a firehose while riding a unicycle. This blog is my attempt to slow down, process what I'm learning, and share thoughts with people who *get it* – the fellow geeks who understand that perfect indentation is its own reward.

## What I'm Into

### 🤖 AI & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence isn't just hype – it's the most exciting technological shift of our generation. From transformer models to neural architecture search, I'm obsessed with how machines learn, reason, and sometimes hallucinate in fascinating ways. Expect posts about:

- Latest AI papers I'm reading
- Experiments with LLMs and generative models
- Practical ML projects and gotchas
- The ethics and philosophy of artificial minds

### ⛓️ Web3 & Decentralization

Say what you want about the crypto bros, but the underlying tech? **Genuinely revolutionary.** Blockchain, smart contracts, decentralized protocols – we're watching the internet's infrastructure get rewritten in real time.

I'll be writing about:
- Building dApps and smart contracts
- The real utility vs. the noise
- Decentralized identity and data ownership
- Why Web3 matters (when it's not being used for JPEG monkeys)

<img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1518770660439-4636190af475?w=800&h=450&fit=crop" alt="Close-up of a computer chip with blue lighting" style="width: 100%; border-radius: 12px; margin: 2rem 0;">

### 🔧 The Little Things

Here's the truth: not every innovation needs to change the world. Some just need to make *your* world slightly better. That's the stuff I love:

- **Dev tools** that make you productive (and the ones that don't)
- **Automation scripts** that save 10 seconds but feel like magic
- **Quality of life tech** – mechanical keyboards, multi-monitor setups, that one VS Code extension you can't live without
- **Dotfiles**, configs, and the art of tinkering

## What to Expect

This isn't a corporate blog. There's no content calendar, no SEO optimization, no "10x developer" hot takes designed to go viral.

Just one engineer sharing what they're learning, building, and thinking about.

Some posts will be technical deep-dives. Some will be quick tips. Some might be late-night ramblings about why we build things in the first place.
